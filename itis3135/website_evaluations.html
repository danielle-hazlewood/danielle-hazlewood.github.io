Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple's website provides a modern feel to their products. The way the website is set up it feels like a luxury experience while also allowing the customer to see all the information they provide.

  • Contrast: Apple using contrast to their advantage. On the home page they use the colors of the phone as the background but keep it lighter in order to make them stand out.
  • Repetition: All the font sizes and placements are relative to the block that they have each of their products in. They also keep the paragraphs short and straight to the point keeping the page uncrowded.
  • Alignment: Apple uses boxes to keep organized and all relevant information together. While in the smaller boxes there is different information between them the separation allows for an easy understanding.
  • Proximity: The proximity of the elements are spaced the same on all sides and across the website. This gives a balanced website for the user to look at.
